Trying Food Hobby

A collection of delicious food I have tried during my travels and daily life. From sushi to street food, each experience was memorable and exciting.

Why I Enjoy Trying Food

Trying food is one of my favourite ways to experience a new place and culture. It’s exciting to discover new flavours, textures, and combinations that I don’t usually eat in my everyday routine.

I enjoy exploring food spots, comparing dishes from different countries, and collecting memories through unique meals and street food experiences.

  • Trying local specialties
  • Unique drinks & desserts
  • Exploring new restaurants
