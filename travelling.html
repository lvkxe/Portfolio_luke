Travelling Hobby

A collection of my travel experiences captured through photography. These images reflect the different places I have visited and the incredible adventures I have had since 2023.

Why I Enjoy Travelling

Travelling lets me experience different cultures, environments, and ways of life. It helps me reset, gain new perspectives, and collect memories through places and moments that feel completely different from everyday routine.

I enjoy exploring cities and nature, taking photos, and learning something new from each destination — even small details like food, architecture, and local lifestyle.

What I Like

  • Exploring new places
  • Capturing photos & scenery
  • Trying local food
  • Culture & city vibes

Phuket

A beach lover's paradise, Phuket offers breathtaking views, crystal-clear water, and exciting activities.

Beijing

A trip to China’s capital, full of rich history, culture, and the majestic Great Wall of China.

Shanghai

A bustling metropolis that blends modernity with tradition, Shanghai is a city of contrasts and vibrant energy.

Bangkok

A city that never sleeps, Bangkok is known for its vibrant street life, cultural landmarks, and bustling markets.

Taipei

A vibrant city known for its lively street food scene, night markets, and iconic Taipei 101 skyscraper.

Melbourne

A cultural hub known for its arts scene, coffee culture, and beautiful coastal scenery.

Bali

An Indonesian paradise known for its forested volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, beaches, and coral reefs.

Kuala Lumpur

The vibrant capital of Malaysia, known for its modern skyline dominated by the iconic Petronas Twin Towers.
